Q: In a sentence {A brown purse is sitting on a green bench}, print paraphrased input sentences.
A: ‘purse’ is changed to ‘bag’. ‘sitting on’ changed to ‘perched atop’. ‘bench’ is changed to ‘seat’. So The Answer is {A brown bag is perched atop a green seat}.
